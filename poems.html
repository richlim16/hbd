Luna

You are not the sun.
My world need not revolve around you, nor my life depend on your existence.
My eyes do not set on fire when I stare at you.
You are not death and life incwrnate rolled into one mess.

No, you are more like the moon.
You are not the necessity for life to exist,
yet when the night comes and you do not, something will feel amiss.
Tides and waves bend at your will, and darkness begone just a bit.

Plainly beautiful in your simplicity
A certain calm and mystery in your eyes
what secrets do you hide in your darker side?
Peace and serenity while the waves around you come crashing down.

You're my moon, my one and only
You are a beauty to behold in the dead of night
you are the star of your own show, the brightest light
My Luna, my queen. My heart beats as the waves crash around me and I'm lost in your eyes.

I Hate Time

I am afraid of time Time makes me afraid I live in fear of what's coming and of what will be.

Time takes me away from what is now and what is real in exchange of what might be.

However, I no longer want to be a slave to an idea which does not exist, to a tomorrow that never comes, so I can live in the beauty of reality.

I want to indulge myself in the buffet of life. To experience its bitter and sour moments and to revel in its sweetness.

To truly bask one's self in the heat of the moment is an act of sacrifice and brevity, rewarded with the fruits of peace and joy the likes of which can only be known by true surrender.
