Sweet Nothings

To be honest, I made this part because 2 options in the menu seemed to little so I wanted to round it up to 3. It's been a while since I wrote a poem for you and I'm constantly scared I won't do better than my previous efforts so that is what this page is for. Thoughts that weren't good enough to turn to poems. My Sweet Nothings.

Entry #1

April 1, 2020

I've been putting this off for way too long but I'm glad I'm writing again. I feel like this is one of the best ways for me to stop overthinking. This is about as much for ou as it is for me, in fact I started writing mostly for my benefit than yours. It keeps me grounded in my thoughts and helps me exercise my memory so I never lose track of what I really want.

Over time I will continue to update this to add more entries and to improve the layout. This is just a way for me to mix my "hobby" and my "other hobby(?)"

Anyway, I hope you're staying safe because I cannot wait to see you soon. All I've been thinking these past few days is what I want to do once I see you again. I wanna go do stuff but I also know I'm pretty lazy so staying in is kewl too hehehe.

Entry #2

April 4, 2020

All I can think about these days is how different things would be if I were stuck with you. How fun it would be to live with you, I always feel more comfortable when I'm with you. Although I hate feeling incompetent or lacking in knowledge, I don't mind acting like a fool when I'm with you, even if I hate feeling vulnerable, if it makes you smile then I don't particularly mind.

I remember what I was like before, it scares me to think that I haven't changed from the person I used to be, yet when I think of you, all I can think of is who I used to be. When I feel myself overthinking, I think of you and I think of who I was before, sometimes I wish we met earlier, I wish I could show you the time when I was full of life, I was so focused on doing and the thought of potential consequences didn't paralyze me as it does now. I feel like that me would've been much more fun.

The thing that keeps me sane, is how much faith I have in you. I'd be lying if I said that you have no reason to worry about me, the sad truth is I am so alienated from my emotions that I even doubt if what I feel is real. In times like that, I find myself relying on you, much more than I would wish, I I calm myself by knowing that you are someone I can rely on. I'm sorry if that puts a burden on you, I just trust you more than I trust myself sometimes.

Entry #4

April 8,2020

It's my birthday today and it kinda sucks that I can't spend it how I want, which is with you. I really liked the little message you made for me though, it was short but simple and in some ways it was exactly what I wanted to hear from you.

I admit that I do enjoy being smothered, I do also really appreciate every little thing you do, and I do not think about how much you show you appreciation, I am just really grateful you've come into my life at all. Eveerytime I think about the little things you do, the little things that make you YOU, I feel so happy and warm that we're together. I feel so grateful that I have someone as understanding as you, as well as someone that I can truly understand.

I really miss you though hehehe I miss listening to your voice and I miss watching videos with you. I miss that feeling of comfort, the feeling that I belong. I belong with you. THe feeling that I can share anything with you. That you can share anything with me. I probably sound like a broken record when I say that I've never felt like I've belonged anywhere else, but it's just so true that it just keeps me in such awe when I think of you, so I thought I should remind you once again how lucky I feel to have you.

I also regret not taking a picture of the two of us because I need more pictures for this website thing I've made :(

I also wish I was more creative in my gift making, I can't sing and I'm not very good at art tho, so I hope you'll enjoy this simple piece of code I've put together for you and pages of me monologueing for your, hopefully, enjoyment.
